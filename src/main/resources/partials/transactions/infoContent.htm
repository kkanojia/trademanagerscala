Information Regarding the application

  • Short selling is not allowed.
  • Buying and selling on same day is considered short selling. So a trade bought on 1st can only be sold on or after 2nd.
  • Trades get settled in a FIFO manner.
  • There is data only for month of August. Dates cannot be other than in August.
  • This has been developed at late nights after office. Please consider that while using it :).
